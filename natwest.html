NATWEST - 2019

Mobile business banking concept

Creating a beta product to trial with customers and then continuously evolve.

My role

Identify a series of key features for NatWest’s business banking mobile app, as a set of key journeys for early formative testing.

Methods

Prototyping, UI design, Concept testing, Design sprints

Team

Product Manager, UI Designer, Domain stakeholders

Outcome

  • Early exploration of key features for mobile business banking offering
  • Partner agency took the early concept to inform an initial beta release in 2019 for trialling with Business customers
  • Identified a massive simplification of payment security that could leverage the unique capabilities of a mobile device
  • A success story for cross-department teams working at pace on a key product offering
  • Facilitated cross-department collaboration of a user-centred approach where all specialisms were able to make a worthy contribution

Background

Existing research had identified that mobile banking was an expectation for NatWest business customers, wanting on-demand convenience and speed of service. Using concept development and continuous delivery in an attempt to achieve greater agility in releasing products and services.

Approach

Conducting a series of sprint-based phases across a 1 month period to initially leverage understanding from different parts of the business, co-creation sessions and feedback on concept development.

Lean discovery

Used a lean hypothesis-driven approach using business customer profiles and secondary research. To move quickly from insight to concept to initial testing with customers.


Persona & co-creation scenarios - stakeholder sessions placed personas in
typical situations to ideate a series of feature ideas.


Concept design

Focused on 2 core banking customers: SME owner and a senior manager in a large organisation. And organisation initiatives for current propositions and technology transformation strategies relevant to mobile business banking.

Sketched wireframes - focusing on the key steps in a journey.


Detailed design - new ideas were merged with existing core banking capabilities to create
a series of user journeys, using NatWest’s existing design system.
